Welcome to my website!

What can you find here?

Here you can find my various projects ranging in discipline from graphic design to engineering, a photo gallery that I use isntead of instagram, a blog which I intend to be an informal medium between project writeups and progress logs, and a menagerie of custom applications, games, and tools for having fun on the web.

How to navigate the site.

Navigating this website is pretty simple, you have already found the home page, here you can find basic information about the website, whats new, and some small-web interlinking through webrings and buttons. The top of all pages has navigation to all other main pages, and the footer of all pages has external links, the sitemap, and copyright information.
In regards to copyright feel free to use any neat web design you find here just make it your own. In general I only safeguard my articles and my photography.

When searching for content on this site you can find long form writeups of my projects and crafts on the Projects page. A photo gallery which I use in leu of instagram can be found on the Gallery Page. Short form updates on my projects as well as small articles can be found on the Blog Page.
Lastly the other page contains many goodies, tools and software made by myself, credits to many great tools made by others, graphics, buttons, stickers, and frames for your own use and pretty much anything I didnt think of when making the website layout.
